title: Glimpse 1.3.1 Release
---

<p>Glimpse 1.3.1 includes timeline bug fixes and new timeline functionality.</p>
<p>Also included are enhancements to the experimental WorldWind integration, including support for drawing in Glimpse with one GeoProjection and reprojecting the scene onto a PlateCarreeProjection for undistorted display on WorldWind.</p>
